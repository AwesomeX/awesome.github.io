Walter's Rebound -

Development Status: Released: August 14, 2020 | Project Started: May 2019 | Engine:

Walter's Rebound is a total recreation of Valve's title Ricochet but with improved mechanics & game-play with additions like bots, new maps, improved graphics, features like an in-game jukebox, game-play modifiers and more. And of course it's multiplayer so you can play online with your friends or strangers.
