About Me

My name is Jason and I've been interested in game development for many years now. I've dedicated a good chunk of my life to staring at computer screens programming, designing, testing and playing various games and projects while loving just about every minute of it.

I have been using Unity for 5+ years and have had experience with many aspects of game development, including prototyping, testing, polishing, releasing, updating, and networking, you name it I've at least dabbled in it.

I've also been a fan of Facepunch for a long time now, and can honestly say I may not have pursued game development as I have if it weren't for FP's forums.
Specifically the programming & gamedev subforums and threads like WAYWO.

Well I suppose that's enough of an introduction for now, lets take a look at what I've actually accomplished!

Frost

Development Time: 3 Months | Engine: Unity
Project Started: May 2015

Frost pitted players against each other in a snowy tundra environment, forcing them to make weapons and equipment from resources found throughout a randomly generated map. With the ultimate objective to be the last man standing.
A unique twist was that when humans died, they came back as animals like crows and wolves which affected gameplay for the ramaining humans.
