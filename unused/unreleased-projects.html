Unreleased Projects

Sometimes things just don't work out. These are games that had potential, but didn't make it in the end.
 

Ogel Island

Development: Postponed | Development Time: 1+ Years | Engine:
Project Started: March 2016
Ogel Island is basically a big GTA style multiplayer sandbox, themed around Lego Island.
Drive cars, jet skis, bikes, buy or build your own house, complete minigames such as pizza delivery and more. With or without friends.
 

Washed Ashore

Development: Dead | Development Time: 4+ Months | Engine:
Project Started: December 2014
Washed Ashore aimed to be a multiplayer recreation of Stranded 2 with new recipes, creatures and things to do.
Now you could beat innocent turtles to death, and eat them with your friends! Or just eat your friends..
 

Frost

Development: Dead | Development Time: 2 Months | Engine:
Project Started: May 2015
Frost pit players against each other in a snowy tundra environment, forcing them to make weapons and equipment from resources found throughout a randomly generated map. With the ultimate objective to be the last man standing.
A unique twist was that when humans died, they came back as animals like crows and wolves which affected gameplay for the ramaining humans.
 

Pig Game

Development: Dead | Development Time: 1 Month | Engine:
Project Started: September 2014
Pig Game was inspired from a mix of bomber man and the Garry's Mod gamemode Ultimate Chimera Hunt.
Players ran around throwing bombs at each other and oinking like a bunch of swine.
